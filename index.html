is the premier online destination to shop exclusive and authentic Vintage and second-hand fashion and luxury ​clothes.​ 

Founded in Paris in 2013, "Paris" is the brainchild of two fashion veterans, Sofia Bernardin and Sabrina Marshall, from VOGUE and Self Service Magazines. Obsessed with iconic vintage, Sabrina and Sofia founded "Paris" with the vision of keeping great fashion in circulation. Every piece on "Paris" is personally curated by our Editors, fully Authenticated by our Experts and restored when necessary by Artisans.
